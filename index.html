Course program

What will your learn

  • Lesson 1. Introduction to Marketing Fundamentals

    Explore the core principles of marketing, including the marketing mix, customer behavior, and the role of marketing in business success. Understand how marketing shapes brand awareness and drives sales.

  • Lesson 2. Market Research and Consumer Insights

    Learn methods for gathering and analyzing market data to understand customer needs and preferences. Discover how to use consumer insights to make informed marketing decisions and tailor your strategies effectively.

  • Lesson 3. Branding and Positioning Strategies

    Understand how to create a strong brand identity and position your product in the market. Explore techniques to differentiate your brand and build lasting connections with your target audience.

  • Lesson 4. Digital Marketing Essentials

    Dive into the key components of digital marketing, including SEO, social media, email campaigns, and content marketing. Learn how to create integrated digital strategies that boost online presence and engagement.

  • Lesson 5. Social Media Marketing and Management

    Explore effective social media tactics to grow your brand’s following and engagement. Learn how to manage social channels, create compelling content, and measure campaign success through analytics.

  • Lesson 6. Advertising and Promotion Techniques

    Examine different advertising channels and promotional tactics to reach your audience. Understand how to craft persuasive messages and use both online and offline advertising to maximize impact.

  • Lesson 7. Marketing Analytics and Performance Measurement

    Discover tools and metrics for tracking marketing performance. Learn how to analyze data to optimize campaigns, improve ROI, and make data-driven decisions for future marketing efforts.

  • Lesson 8. Marketing Strategy and Campaign Planning

    Bring all lessons together to develop a comprehensive marketing strategy. Learn how to plan, execute, and evaluate marketing campaigns that align with business goals and deliver measurable results.
